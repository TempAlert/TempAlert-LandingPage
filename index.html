Our services

TempAlert allows you to control and adjust the temperature of your warehouses and counters in real time from anywhere, ensuring the quality of your products and avoiding losses due to poor storage. At TempAlert we offer you:

Security: Protect the quality of your products, avoiding losses due to improper storage

Fast: Real-time monitoring and control of temperature

Tailor-made solutions: Flexible integration with existing refrigeration and air conditioning systems

Saves time and resources: Automatic temperature monitoring, freeing up time for other important tasks

Easy to use: Intuitive and easy-to-learn user interface, no prior technical knowledge required
